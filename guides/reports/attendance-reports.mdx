Frappe HR provides several attendance and leave reports that give HR teams and managers visibility into employee presence, shift coverage, holiday work, and leave ledger history.

**How to access:** HR module → Reports

## Monthly Attendance Sheet

The Monthly Attendance Sheet is the primary attendance report. It renders a calendar-style grid with one row per employee and one column per day in the selected month.

**Attendance status codes used in the report:**

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| P | Present |
| A | Absent |
| HD | Half Day |
| OD | On Duty |
| WFH | Work From Home |
| L | On Leave |
| H | Holiday |
| WO | Weekly Off |

**Filters:** Month, Year, Company, Department, Employee, Employee Type

<Tip>
Export the Monthly Attendance Sheet to Excel for payroll input. The grid layout makes it easy to spot patterns such as frequent Monday/Friday absences.
</Tip>

## Shift Attendance Report

Shows attendance records filtered and grouped by shift type. Each row corresponds to an employee's attendance on a specific date, with the assigned shift displayed.

Use this report to:
- Verify that auto-attendance from shift check-ins has processed correctly.
- Identify employees who were absent or late on particular shifts.
- Review shift coverage for a specific period.

**Filters:** From Date, To Date, Shift Type, Company, Department, Employee

## Employees Working on a Holiday

Lists attendance records that fall on days marked as holidays in the employee's assigned Holiday List.

Use this report to:
- Identify employees who need compensatory off or overtime pay for holiday work.
- Verify that holiday work was authorized.

**Filters:** Date, Company, Holiday List

<Note>
For this report to return results, employees must have attendance records manually marked or auto-generated via check-in for the holiday date, AND the date must be in their assigned Holiday List.
</Note>

## Employee Hours Utilization Based on Timesheet

Compares actual hours logged in timesheets against each employee's standard working hours for the period. Shows:

- **Total Hours** — Sum of timesheet hours in the period.
- **Billed Hours** — Hours logged against billable projects.
- **Unbilled Hours** — Hours logged as internal or non-billable.
- **% Utilization** — Billed hours as a percentage of total hours.

**Filters:** From Date, To Date, Company, Employee, Project

<Info>
This report requires timesheets to be submitted in Frappe Framework. It reflects hours at the timesheet level, not at the daily attendance level.
</Info>

## Leave Ledger

The Leave Ledger report shows a chronological transaction log for an employee's leave balance. Each row represents a single leave transaction:

| Transaction Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Leave Allocation | Leaves granted via Leave Allocation document. |
| Leave Application | Leaves deducted when a leave application is approved. |
| Leave Encashment | Leaves converted to cash payment. |
| Carry Forward | Leaves carried forward to the next period. |
| Expiry | Leaves that expired at the end of the leave period. |

**Filters:** Employee, Leave Type, From Date, To Date, Transaction Type

Use this report to:
- Reconcile discrepancies between a visible leave balance and expected allocation.
- Trace the exact dates and amounts of each leave transaction.
- Verify that leave expiry has run correctly after a period closes.

## Exporting reports to Excel or CSV

All attendance reports support export to Excel (`.xlsx`) and CSV.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the report">
    Navigate to the report from the HR module Reports list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set filters">
    Select the desired date range, company, department, and any other relevant filters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh">
    Click **Refresh** to load report data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Click the **Export** button (download icon) in the report toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select format">
    Choose **Excel** for a formatted spreadsheet or **CSV** for a plain data file compatible with other tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
For the Monthly Attendance Sheet, the Excel export preserves the grid layout. For list-style reports like the Leave Ledger, CSV is often more convenient for importing into payroll or BI tools.
</Tip>
