---

Frappe HR includes a comprehensive set of built-in reports covering the full employee lifecycle. All reports are accessible from the HR module under **Reports**.

## Employee reports

### Employee Analytics

Displays aggregate employee data such as headcount over time, new hires, and separations. Use this report to monitor workforce growth and attrition trends.

**Filters:** Company, Date Range, Department, Branch, Designation

### Employee Information

A tabular report listing all active employees with key profile fields including name, designation, department, date of joining, and contact details. Useful for audits and bulk data exports.

**Filters:** Company, Department, Branch, Designation, Status

### Employee Birthday

Lists employees with upcoming birthdays within a selected date range. Use this to plan recognition activities or verify that birthday reminders are firing correctly.

**Filters:** Month, Company

### Employee Exits

Shows employees who have been separated within a given period, including their last working day, reason for leaving, and exit interview status. Use this to track attrition and monitor offboarding completion.

**Filters:** Company, Date Range, Department

### Employee Advance Summary

Shows outstanding employee advances — the amount disbursed, claimed against expense claims, and the remaining balance. Use this to identify employees with unclaimed advances.

**Filters:** Company, Employee, Status

## Leave reports

### Employee Leave Balance

Shows the current leave balance for each employee broken down by leave type. Displays leave allocated, taken, and remaining. Essential for leave audits at the end of a leave period.

**Filters:** Date, Company, Employee, Leave Type, Department

### Employee Leave Balance Summary

A pivot-style summary version of the leave balance report. Displays all leave types as columns so you can compare balances across the organization at a glance.

**Filters:** Date, Company, Department, Employee

### Leave Ledger

Shows a chronological log of all leave transactions for an employee — allocations, applications, encashments, and expirations. Use this to trace the history of a specific employee's leave balance.

**Filters:** Employee, Leave Type, From Date, To Date, Transaction Type

## Attendance reports

### Monthly Attendance Sheet

A grid report showing daily attendance status for all employees in a given month. Each cell shows the attendance status for that day: Present, Absent, Half Day, On Leave, Holiday, or Weekly Off.

**Filters:** Month, Year, Company, Department, Employee

<Tip>
The Monthly Attendance Sheet can be exported to Excel for payroll processing or HR audits. Use the **Export** button at the top of the report.
</Tip>

### Shift Attendance Report

Shows attendance records grouped by shift. Useful for organizations running multiple shifts to identify attendance patterns and gaps per shift.

**Filters:** Shift Type, From Date, To Date, Company, Department

### Employees Working on a Holiday

Lists employees who have attendance records marked on days that are holidays in their assigned Holiday List. Use this to process compensation or overtime for holiday work.

**Filters:** Date, Company, Holiday List

### Employee Hours Utilization Based on Timesheet

Compares billed vs. unbilled hours from timesheets against each employee's standard working hours. Useful for professional services organizations tracking utilization rates.

**Filters:** From Date, To Date, Company, Project, Employee

## Appraisal reports

### Appraisal Overview

Provides a summary of appraisal status across the organization for a given appraisal cycle. Shows which employees have completed self-appraisals, received ratings, and had final appraisals approved.

**Filters:** Appraisal Cycle, Company, Department

## Recruitment reports

### Recruitment Analytics

Tracks the recruitment funnel from job openings through applicants to offers. Shows metrics like time-to-hire and applicant conversion rates per job opening and department.

**Filters:** Company, From Date, To Date, Department, Designation

## Expense reports

### Unpaid Expense Claim

Lists submitted and approved expense claims that have not yet been paid. Use this to identify overdue reimbursements and process payments.

**Filters:** Company, Employee, Department, From Date, To Date

### Vehicle Expenses

Summarizes vehicle-related expense claims by employee and vehicle. Use this for fleet cost tracking and reimbursement audits.

**Filters:** Company, From Date, To Date, Employee

### Project Profitability

Combines timesheet hours and expense data against project revenue to calculate per-project profitability. Links HR costs to ERPNext project accounting.

**Filters:** Company, Project, From Date, To Date

### Daily Work Summary Replies

Shows replies received from employees in response to Daily Work Summary emails. Use this to review team activity logs without opening individual summary records.

**Filters:** Daily Work Summary Group, From Date, To Date

## Exporting reports

All Frappe HR reports can be exported to Excel (`.xlsx`) or CSV format.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run the report">
    Apply your desired filters and click **Refresh** to generate the report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export">
    Click the **Export** button (download icon) in the top-right toolbar of the report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose format">
    Select **Excel** or **CSV** from the export options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    The file downloads to your browser's default download location.
  </Step>
</Steps>
