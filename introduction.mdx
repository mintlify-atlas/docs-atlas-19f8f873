## What is Frappe HR?

Frappe HR (formerly the HR modules in ERPNext) is a standalone HRMS application with 13+ modules covering every aspect of human resource management:

- **Employee Lifecycle** — Onboarding, transfers, promotions, and separation
- **Leave & Attendance** — Leave policies, balance tracking, shift management, and geolocation check-in
- **Payroll & Taxation** — Salary structures, tax slabs, payroll entries, and compliance reports
- **Expense Claims** — Multi-level approval workflows and integration with accounting
- **Performance Management** — Appraisal cycles, goal tracking, and 360° feedback
- **Recruitment** — Job requisitions through offer letters
- **Mobile PWA** — Apply for leaves, check in/out, and manage expenses from your phone

<Info>
Frappe HR requires [ERPNext](https://erpnext.com) and [Frappe Framework](https://frappeframework.com) as dependencies. It integrates seamlessly with ERPNext for accounting, project management, and other business functions.
</Info>
