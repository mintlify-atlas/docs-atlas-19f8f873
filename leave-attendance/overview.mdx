Frappe HR's leave and attendance system covers the full lifecycle of time-off management — from defining what kinds of leave exist, to allocating balances, processing applications, and automatically deriving attendance from employee check-in logs.

## How the system fits together

Leave management and attendance are separate concerns that share data:

- **Leave management** tracks balances and approved absences using a ledger model. Every allocation, application, and encashment creates a Leave Ledger Entry.
- **Attendance** tracks whether an employee was present, absent, or on leave for each working day. Attendance records are created manually, via bulk upload, or automatically from shift check-in logs.

The two systems are linked when a leave application is approved — an attendance record with status **On Leave** is created for each day of the approved leave.

## Leave setup sequence

Complete setup in this order before employees can apply for leave.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create leave types">
    Define each category of leave — Annual, Sick, Compensatory, and so on — with rules for carry-forward, encashment, and accrual. See [Leave types](/leave-attendance/leave-types).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a leave period">
    A Leave Period defines the date range (typically a calendar or financial year) over which allocations are valid. Go to **HR > Leave Period > New**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a leave policy">
    A Leave Policy groups leave types with their annual entitlement quantities. One policy can be reused across many employees. See [Leave policies](/leave-attendance/leave-policy).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign the leave policy">
    A Leave Policy Assignment links a policy to one or more employees for a specific leave period. This triggers automatic Leave Allocation creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify leave allocations">
    Each Leave Allocation record holds the actual balance for one employee, one leave type, and one period. Review allocations before employees start applying.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Employees submit leave applications">
    Once allocations exist, employees apply for leave via the Leave Application form or the mobile PWA. See [Leave applications](/leave-attendance/leave-application).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Attendance setup sequence

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a holiday list">
    Define weekly off days and public holidays. See [Holiday lists](/leave-attendance/holiday-list).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign the holiday list">
    Attach the holiday list to employees or departments so attendance calculations respect non-working days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure shifts (optional)">
    If your organization uses shift-based work, create Shift Types with timing and auto-attendance rules, then assign employees to shifts. See [Shift management](/leave-attendance/shift-management).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark attendance">
    Attendance can be marked manually, uploaded in bulk via CSV, or generated automatically from Employee Checkin logs. See [Marking attendance](/leave-attendance/attendance-marking).
  </Step>
</Steps>
