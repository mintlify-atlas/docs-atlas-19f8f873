title: "Job applicants"
description: "Track job applications from any source, manage applicant statuses, and handle employee referrals."

A Job Applicant record captures everything about a candidate's application — their contact details, resume, cover letter, the source of their application, and their current status in the pipeline.

## How applicants apply

Applications can enter the system in two ways:

**Via the website form** — When a Job Opening is published, candidates can submit their details through the public application form. A Job Applicant record is created automatically from the form submission.

**Manual entry** — HR staff can create a Job Applicant record directly in **HR > Recruitment > Job Applicant** by clicking **New**.

<Note>
Creating a Job Applicant against a **Closed** Job Opening is not allowed. The system raises an error on insert if the linked opening has a `Closed` status.
</Note>

## Required fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Applicant Name** | Full name of the candidate. If left blank, it is guessed from the email address |
| **Email Address** | Primary contact email. Used as the document name (with a numeric suffix if the same email applies again) |
| **Status** | Current pipeline stage (see below) |

## Applicant statuses

Job Applicant status drives workflow routing and reporting. The available statuses are:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Open** | Application received; under initial review |
| **Replied** | HR has responded to the applicant |
| **Hold** | Application is paused, pending a decision |
| **Accepted** | Applicant has been selected; a Job Offer will be created |
| **Rejected** | Application has been declined |

Status updates also propagate to linked documents. When a Job Offer linked to an applicant is set to **Accepted** or **Rejected**, the Job Applicant status is updated to match automatically.

## Applicant sources

The **Source** field links to the **Job Applicant Source** master, letting you track where each application originated. Common sources include:

- Website
- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Referral (Employee Referral)
- Campus
- Walk-in

When the source is **Employee Referral**, a **Source Name** field appears to record the referring employee.

## Employee Referral workflow

Frappe HR includes a dedicated **Employee Referral** document that tracks referrals made by existing employees.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Employee submits a referral">
    An employee submits an **Employee Referral** document nominating a candidate for a specific role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Job Applicant is linked">
    When HR creates a Job Applicant and links it to the Employee Referral in the **Employee Referral** field, the referral status updates automatically based on the applicant's progress:

    | Applicant status | Referral status |
    |---|---|
    | Open / Replied / Hold | In Process |
    | Accepted | Accepted |
    | Rejected | Rejected |
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The **Employee Referral** field on a Job Applicant is read-only and is set by the system when a Job Applicant is created from an Employee Referral document.
</Info>

## Resume and cover letter

The **Resume** section holds:

- **Cover Letter** — free-text field for the candidate's covering note
- **Resume Attachment** — file attachment for an uploaded CV
- **Resume Link** — URL to an online profile or hosted resume

## Salary expectation

Record the candidate's expected compensation in the **Salary Expectation** section using **Currency**, **Lower Range**, and **Upper Range** fields.

## Applicant rating

HR staff can assign an **Applicant Rating** (star rating) to each applicant for quick shortlisting. This rating is visible in the list view.

## Linking to interviews

From a Job Applicant record, click the **Create Interview** button to schedule an interview. The system:

1. Prompts you to select an **Interview Round**
2. Validates that the round's designation matches the applicant's designation (if set)
3. Creates a new Interview document pre-filled with the applicant's details and the round's interviewer panel

You can view all interviews scheduled for an applicant in the **Interview Details** dashboard section.
